BLACK AND WHITE

Tools: Illustrator

This is a reflection poster of viewing the world in black and white. Black and white signifies a person’s negativity from the abundance of stress or horrific event he or she may have encountered in their lives. Due to their previous encounters, some people are blinded by their negativities, and it prevents them from seeing life filled with colors. This poster shows that, in time, people will heal and the colors, which signifies hope, will be shown again to the blinded.
